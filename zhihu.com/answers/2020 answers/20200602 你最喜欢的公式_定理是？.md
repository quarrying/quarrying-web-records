贝叶斯定理. 概率统计和机器学习中不可回避的存在.

$$P\left( {{B_i}|A} \right) = \frac{{P\left( {A|{B_i}} \right)P\left( {{B_i}}
\right)}}{{P\left( A \right)}} = \frac{{P\left( {A|{B_i}} \right)P\left(
{{B_i}} \right)}}{{\sum\nolimits_n {P\left( {A|{B_n}} \right)P\left( {{B_n}}
\right)} }}$$
